## Biology and Pharmaceuticals (BioPharma) Use Cases
| ID | Use Case | Description |
|-|-|-|
|1.  | **Compound Safety** | Surface insights from data to improve decision making. |
|2. | **Compound Synthesis** | Streamlined ELN input and insights save time for our synthetic chemists. |
| 3. | **Accelerating Target Discovery & Validation** | Identify and prioritize new targets using custom ML models to predict viability based on disease biology, accelerating preclinical drug discovery timelines. |  
| 4. | **Smart Screening & Lead Optimization** | Predict molecular binding affinities and toxicology risks by filtering data. Streamline compound screening and optimize workflows, reducing IND filing time with a unified platform. |  
| 5. | **AI-Driven Biomarker Discovery** | Transform unstructured imaging, notes, and omics data into structured data for clinical trials and biomarker discovery. Fine-tune AI models in radiology, pathology, and omics to detect biomarkers. |  
| 6. | **Virtual Trials** | Use Healthcare AI models to stratify patient cohorts based on their response to immunotherapies. |  
| 7. | **Lab Automation** | AI-driven autonomous orchestration of high-throughput chemistry assay workflow. |
| 8. | **Generative Chemistry & Optimization** | AI-driven decision making to identify compounds with the highest overall value. |
