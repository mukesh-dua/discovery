## Chemistry and Material Science (CMS) Use Cases
| ID | Use Case | Description |
|-|-|-|
|1. | **Molecular Screening** | Filter through molecular databases to identify molecular solutions that meet application performance criteria.|
|2. |**Molecular Discovery** |Generatively explore novel design spaces to identify molecules that meet defined application performance criteria.|
|3. | **Reaction Exploration** | Explore reaction networks to understand chemical synthesis and degradation mechanisms.|
|4. |**Materials Discovery** |Explore enumerative combinations of relevant crystal structures and elements that meet defined application performance criteria.|
|5. |**Materials Mixture Optimization** |Identify combinations of components that demonstrate optimum application performance.|
|6. |**Quantum Refinement** |Target Quantum Computer resources to study chemical and materials systems limited by computational accuracy.|
